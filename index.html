About Us

Born from the heart of India's farmland.

At V Pack Pulp Products, we specialize in high-quality, sustainable, and eco-friendly paper egg boxes designed to protect and preserve eggs during transportation and storage.

Using recycled materials and biodegradable pulp, our egg cartons offer an environmentally responsible alternative to plastic packaging.

Whether you're a small farm, a grocery chain, or a packaging supplier — our custom-designed boxes are tailored to meet your needs while promoting sustainability.
